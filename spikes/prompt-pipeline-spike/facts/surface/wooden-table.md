A flat horizontal wooden surface, lightly worn from use. Visible grain runs
roughly parallel to one of its long edges, with subtle variation in tone from
lighter sapwood streaks to darker heartwood patches. The finish is matte —
no high gloss, no varnish glare. Small marks accumulate near the edges where
things are set down most often; the center remains the cleanest area. The
wood reads as warm-toned, well-loved, with the patina of regular domestic use
rather than newness or restoration. The surface absorbs light rather than
reflecting it sharply.
