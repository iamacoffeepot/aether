The object is intact — no chips, cracks, fractures, scratches, or
visible damage. Surface contours follow the original form without
interruption. Glaze, finish, or coating is unbroken and continuous.
The object reads as carefully kept rather than worn through; signs of
use may be present (slight thinning at high-touch points, gentle
softening of edges) but never structural compromise.
